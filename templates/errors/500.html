Error 500

A 500 error is like walking into a room and finding everything in chaos. On the web, when you see a 500 error, it means that something went wrong on the server and it can't fulfill your request. It could be because the server is overloaded, there's a bug in the code, or some other internal issue. It's as if you're trying to have a conversation, but the other person is too overwhelmed to respond coherently.
